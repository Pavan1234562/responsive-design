About Pirates

A pirate in the world of One Piece is anyone who raises a Jolly Roger, whether they commit an act of piracy or not. This is not, however, usually enough to give anyone a bounty, but is enough to be arrested by the Marines. Bandits are said to be the opposite of pirates.

Their Mission

The One Piece is the driving goal of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, as well as that of multiple other pirates, who all seek to claim the treasure in order to become the next Pirate King, following Roger's dying words at his execution.

Luffy's Dream

While Luffy's immediate goal is to find the One Piece treasure and be recognized as the new Pirate King, the manga has recently revealed that this is only a means to something else that remains secret, but One Piece Film: Red just dropped a huge hint on what Luffy's real dream is.
